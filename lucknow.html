Lucknow Event Planning FAQs

Find answers to the most common questions about booking decorators and planners in our city.

Our listed event planners and balloon decorators cover all major areas in Lucknow, including Gomti Nagar, Aliganj, Hazratganj, Indira Nagar, Ashiyana, and Mahanagar. Many also serve the outskirts up to 30km.

Basic room decorations start around ₹1,499. Premium setups for banquet halls with themes, neon signs, and floral arrangements generally range from ₹3,500 to ₹10,000 depending on your requirements.

Yes! Many of our top-rated partners in Lucknow offer emergency or same-day decoration services. Just use the "Call Now" or "WhatsApp" button on their listing to check immediate availability.

Generally, travel within the main city limits of Lucknow is included. If your venue is far out (like BKT or towards the Purvanchal Expressway), a small transportation fee might apply.
